_Shortcuts_

- **Wiki Style Links:** Two Big Brackets [[]]

- ## Heading 2:
	  - With two ##.
- ### Heading 3 :

- Bold
	  - With **Double Astrik** (*)
- Italic
	  - With _Double Underscore (_)
- Create Link with shortcut
	Hold (Cmd + K) to add [link.]
